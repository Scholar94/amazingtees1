Our Educational Programs

Pre School

Our Pre School program provides a nurturing environment for children ages 2-4, focusing on early development through play-based learning.

Kindergarten

Our Kindergarten program prepares children ages 4-6 for primary education with a balanced curriculum of academics and social development.

Nursery

Our Nursery program offers a safe and stimulating environment for toddlers to explore, learn and develop essential skills.

Primary School

Our Primary School program provides a strong academic foundation for children in grades 1-6 with a comprehensive curriculum.

High School

Our High School program prepares students for higher education and future careers with specialized courses and college counseling.
