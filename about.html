Our Mission & Vision

At Amazing Tees Schools, our mission is to provide a transformative educational experience that nurtures curiosity, fosters creativity, and builds character in students from their earliest years through adolescence.

We believe in educating the whole child - intellectually, socially, emotionally, and physically - through a balanced approach that combines academic excellence with character development.

Our vision is to create a community of lifelong learners who are prepared to meet the challenges of a changing world with confidence, compassion, and critical thinking skills.

Nursery Program

Our nursery program provides the youngest learners (ages 1-2) with a warm, secure environment that feels like a home away from home. We focus on developing trust, security, and early sensory experiences.

  • Safe, nurturing environment with low child-to-teacher ratios
  • Focus on sensory development and motor skills
  • Gentle introduction to social interactions
  • Regular communication with parents about development
Learn More

Preschool Program

Our preschool program (ages 2-3) builds on the foundation of the nursery years with more structured play and early learning activities that prepare children for kindergarten.

  • Introduction to letters, numbers, colors, and shapes
  • Development of social skills through group activities
  • Focus on independence and self-help skills
  • Creative expression through art, music, and movement
Learn More

Kindergarten Program

Our kindergarten program (ages 4-5) provides a balanced approach to learning that combines play-based activities with more structured academic preparation for primary school.

  • Phonics-based reading readiness program
  • Introduction to mathematical concepts
  • Science exploration and discovery
  • Development of critical thinking and problem-solving skills
Learn More

Primary School

Our primary school (Grades 1-6) builds a strong academic foundation while encouraging curiosity and a love of learning through engaging, hands-on experiences.

  • Comprehensive curriculum meeting national standards
  • Specialized instruction in art, music, and physical education
  • Technology integration across all subject areas
  • Character education and leadership opportunities
Learn More

High School

Our high school (Grades 7-12) prepares students for college and beyond through rigorous academics, diverse extracurricular opportunities, and personalized guidance.

  • College preparatory curriculum with Advanced Placement courses
  • STEM specialization programs
  • Arts and humanities tracks
  • College counseling and career guidance
